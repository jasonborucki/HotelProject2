Hotel Cancellation Visualizations


Key Findings

If customers did not make special requests, it was nearly a 50-50 split of cancellations to non-cancellations.

However, once special requests were made, this dropped to around a 23% cancellation rate.

A similar story can be said with car parking. If a customer did not require car parking, the cancellation rate was around 40%

However, with car parking, this resulted in 0 % cancellation rate across 7000 total reservations.

The biggest finding, however, was how far in advance reservations were made. This line linear regression model shows that the more in advance a reservation was made, the higher likelihood there was of a cancellation. For example, if the lead_time_range was a 5, this meant that a reservation was made more than 500 days in advance. Over 600 reservations were made this far in advance and resulted in an 85% cancellation rate. Compare that to around a 27% cancellation rate to those reservations made within 100 days of the date the reservation was made.

The results of the model testing. 74% accuracy rate that used 50% of the data set as the training set and 50% for the testing set.
